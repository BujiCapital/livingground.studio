The Community

Nine extraordinary homes in the Scotts Valley hills.

Sandraya Heights is a collection of nine luxury residences nestled in the hills above Scotts Valley, each set on a generous half-acre lot. The homes range from 3,800 to 4,000 square feet, with four to five bedrooms and four-and-a-half bathrooms designed for the way families actually live today. Every home includes solar power as standard, reflecting a commitment to modern, sustainable living.

The setting is exceptional. Surrounded by towering redwoods with hiking trails at your doorstep, Sandraya Heights sits just fifteen minutes from the beach. It is the rare development that combines the privacy and natural beauty of the Santa Cruz Mountains with genuine proximity to the coast and to Silicon Valley.

Nine distinct floor plans anchor the community: Willowbend, Altamonte, Contempo, Monte Sereno, Oakridge, Sagefield, Moderna, Monte Luz, and Heartwood. Each is architecturally distinctive, but they share a common quality: these are homes designed to be lived in deeply, with indoor-outdoor connections that deserve a landscape to match.
