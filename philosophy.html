We design landscapes the way nature designs ecosystems

Every Living Ground project is guided by twelve design principles — a framework for creating landscapes that are beautiful, productive, and built to last generations.

Three commitments before we draw a single line

These aren't slogans. They're the foundation of every design decision we make.

♢

Care for the land

Every landscape we build improves the soil, conserves water, and creates habitat. Your backyard becomes part of the living ecosystem.

♡

Care for the people

A landscape should nourish the family that lives in it — fresh food, outdoor living, beauty, and a place to gather.

∞

Share the abundance

A well-designed landscape produces more than any family needs. Share the harvest, return surplus to the soil.
